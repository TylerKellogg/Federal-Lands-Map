Reclamation Facilities — RISE

Live from Reclamation's RISE catalog: named dams/reservoirs, hydropower plants, and pumping plants the Bureau operates (e.g. Hoover, Grand Coulee). These are point facilities — distinct from the PAD-US surface polygons. When shown, clicking a Reclamation surface unit also lists the facilities that fall within it.

Mining — Federal Claims (BLM)

Live from BLM's Mineral & Land Records System: all not-closed mining claim cases on BLM-administered minerals. Claims load when zoomed in (≈1:500k or closer) — they are far too numerous to draw statewide.

Major Mines — MSHA

Min workforce
Snapshot of the MSHA Mines dataset (Jul 2026): active mines, ≥50 employees.

Wildfire — Active Incidents

Live from NIFC/WFIGS (interagency system of record; refreshed ~5 min). Mapped fire perimeters only — the print briefing counts all current incidents statewide, including those too new or small to have a mapped perimeter.
